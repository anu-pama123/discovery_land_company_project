    Supproting the community's children

    It’s important that our private communities interact with, and actively participate in, the wider neighborhood.

    That’s why we established The Discovery Land Company Foundation; which primarily supports disadvantaged children in shelters and in foster care and donates to carefully vetted, fully trusted charities.

    It’s all about giving these kids a better start in life, and giving them a little extra help to make the most of their potential.
